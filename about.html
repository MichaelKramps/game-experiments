About Kramps Games

Kramps Games is named after me, Michael Kramps, the sole developer. My games can be played directly in your browser with any device.

All my games are free of advertisements. The free games require no sign up. The full games require a Google account to log in, and each game can be purchased in full for a one time price.

I tend to build low stress games, which often means turn-based, or untimed game speed. I also enjoy strategy in games more than real time skill.
